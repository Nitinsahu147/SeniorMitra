Join Our Engaging Virtual
Workshops

Our virtual workshops offer engaging and interactive experiences from the comfort of your home. Explore new skills, hobbies, and educational topics while connecting with experts and fellow seniors. Enjoy diverse sessions that cater to your interests and enhance your daily life.







How to Participate

Register for upcoming workshops through our easy-to-use sign-up process. Simply select the workshop that interests you, enter your details, and you’ll receive a confirmation and instructions on how to join.

Join the Workshop

On the day of the workshop, use the provided link to join the virtual session. Be sure to log in a few minutes early to get settled and test your connection.

Benefits of Participation

Gain new knowledge and skills in areas that interest you. Meet like-minded seniors and build new friendships through shared interests and activities.




Discover, Learn, and Connect Through Our Virtual Workshops

     

Join our virtual workshops to explore new interests, enhance your skills, and engage with a supportive community. These interactive sessions cover a wide range of topics, from health and wellness to creative arts and technology. Participate from the comfort of your home and connect with experts and fellow seniors in a vibrant learning environment designed to enrich your life.


Digital Photography Basics

Date: October 6, 2024
Time: 2:00 AM - 3:30 PM
Location: Online (Zoom)
Dive into the world of digital photography with this comprehensive workshop designed for beginners. Learn the essentials of operating your camera, including key settings and techniques to capture stunning images. Explore composition tips, lighting strategies, and basic photo editing to enhance your photographs. Whether you're using a smartphone or a digital camera, this workshop will help you develop your skills and take your photography to the next level.


Healthy Cooking for Seniors

Date: October 15, 2024
Time: 1:00 PM - 2:30 PM
Location: Community Center, 123 Main Street, Indore
Join us for a delicious and informative workshop focused on healthy cooking tailored specifically for seniors. Discover nutritious recipes that cater to common dietary needs and preferences. Learn practical cooking techniques, meal planning strategies, and how to incorporate more fruits, vegetables, and whole grains into your diet. This interactive session will provide you with the skills and confidence to prepare meals that are both tasty and beneficial for your overall health.


Exploring Creative Writing

Date: October 18, 2024
Time: 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM
Location: Senior Activity Hall, 456 Elm Street, Bhopal
Tap into your creative potential in this engaging creative writing workshop. Whether you're interested in writing fiction, poetry, or personal essays, this session offers valuable techniques and prompts to inspire your writing journey. Learn how to develop characters, build compelling narratives, and express your thoughts and emotions effectively. Introduction to Online Shopping

Date: October 22, 2024
Time: 11:00 AM - 12:30 PM
Location: Wellness Studio, 789 Oak Avenue, Indore
Familiarize yourself with the world of online shopping in this practical workshop designed for seniors.
