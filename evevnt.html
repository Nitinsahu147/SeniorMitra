Exciting Events Just Around the Corner

     

Explore our Event Calendar to discover a variety of upcoming activities designed especially for seniors. Whether you're interested in social gatherings, educational workshops, or recreational events, there's something for everyone. Stay engaged and make the most of each day by joining these enriching experiences, connecting with others, and enjoying new opportunities to learn and grow.


Virtual Coffee Chat with Friends

Date: September 20, 2024
Time: 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM
Location: Online (Zoom)
Start your day with a virtual coffee chat where you can reconnect with old friends and meet new ones from the comfort of your home. This informal gathering offers a warm and friendly atmosphere to share stories, discuss topics of interest, and build meaningful connections.


Healthy Living Workshop: Nutrition Tips

Date: September 25, 2024
Time: 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM
Location: Community Center, 123 Main Street, Indore
Join us for an informative workshop on healthy eating tailored specifically for seniors. A certified nutritionist will provide practical tips on maintaining a balanced diet, understanding nutritional needs, and making delicious, healthful meals. Bring your questions and learn how to enhance your well-being through better nutrition.


Classic Movie Night

Date: September 28, 2024
Time: 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM
Location: Senior Activity Hall, 456 Elm Street, Bhopal
Description: Enjoy a delightful evening watching a classic film with fellow movie enthusiasts. This event offers a great opportunity to relive old favorites, share memories, and engage in lively discussion about the film. Popcorn and refreshments will be provided for a truly enjoyable experience.


Gentle Yoga for Seniors

Date: October 02, 2024
Time: 9:00 AM - 10:00 AM
Location: Wellness Studio, 789 Oak Avenue, Indore
Description: Participate in a gentle yoga session designed specifically for seniors, focusing on slow stretches, breathing techniques, and relaxation exercises. This class aims to improve flexibility, balance, and overall well-being in a supportive and comfortable environment. No prior yoga experience is needed.


Art and Craft Club Meeting

Date: October 05, 2024
Time: 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM
Location: Arts & Crafts Center, 321 Maple Drive, Jabalpur
Description: Unleash your creativity at our Art and Craft Club meeting. Bring your own art supplies or use the materials provided to explore various crafts, from painting and knitting to sculpture and mixed media. This event is a wonderful way to engage in creative expression, learn new techniques, and enjoy the company of fellow artists.
